CrediProcure

Decentralized B2B invoice financing on Creditcoin testnet, connecting vendors with DeFi investors through on-chain RWA tokenization, funding, repayment, and yield tracking.

Creditcoin Testnet
DeFi + RWA
Validated on March 8, 2026

Problem

SMEs face a global trade finance gap and often wait 30 to 90 days to receive payment. Traditional invoice financing is slow, opaque, and difficult to access, while DeFi investors have limited access to stable real-world-backed yield opportunities.

Solution

CrediProcure turns unpaid B2B invoices into tokenized on-chain assets. Vendors create and mint invoices as RWAs, investors fund them with stablecoins, and repayments plus investor yield are tracked directly on Creditcoin testnet.

Core Flow

1. Create Vendor creates an invoice
2. Mint Invoice is minted as an RWA NFT
3. Fund Investor funds the invoice on-chain
4. Repay Vendor repays principal plus yield

Product Modules

  • Vendor portal for invoice creation, RWA minting, and repayment
  • Investor marketplace for direct invoice funding
  • Yield portfolio based on live funding and repayment events
  • On-chain credit history for funding and repayment records
  • Liquidity pool flow for testnet stablecoin deposits and withdrawals

Protocol Architecture

  • InvoiceNFT.sol: ERC-721 invoice tokenization
  • LendingPool.sol: direct funding, pool liquidity, repayment accounting
  • MockStablecoin.sol: public testnet demo stablecoin
  • Frontend: React + TypeScript + Ethers.js + Vite
