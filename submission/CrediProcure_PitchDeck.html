CrediProcure | Pitch Deck | March 2026

On-chain invoice financing for SMEs and investors.

CrediProcure transforms unpaid B2B invoices into tokenized RWAs on Creditcoin, enabling faster vendor liquidity and transparent investor yield.

Category

DeFi + RWA on Creditcoin testnet.

Deployment

Live app + deployed smart contracts on testnet.

Core Asset

Invoice NFT (ERC-721) as on-chain real-world asset.

Built by Pandu Dargah.

Problem and Opportunity

Trade finance is still slow, opaque, and costly.

What is broken

  • SMEs wait 30-90 days for invoice settlement.
  • Traditional factoring is paperwork-heavy and intermediary-dependent.
  • Funding terms and repayment visibility are often unclear.
  • DeFi investors lack transparent real-world-backed yield products.

Market context

$3.6T

Global trade finance gap.

30-90d

Typical invoice delay.

SME

Primary borrower segment.

RWA

Investor demand for auditable yield.

Solution

Tokenize invoice. Fund on-chain. Repay transparently.

Step 1 Create

Vendor creates invoice with amount, due date, and yield.

Step 2 Mint

Invoice is minted as ERC-721 RWA NFT on Creditcoin.

Step 3 Fund

Investor funds directly or via liquidity pool.

Step 4 Repay

Repayment updates credit history and portfolio yield.

Vendor Flow

Vendor journey from invoice setup to repayment tracking.

Compliance and Credit

KYB validation and event-driven credit history.

Why this matters

  • Improves lender confidence with transparent borrower history.
  • Creates reusable on-chain signals for future risk scoring.
  • Supports compliance-ready progression toward production rollout.
Investor Flow

Investor experience across discovery, allocation, and yield tracking.

Architecture

Three-contract protocol on Creditcoin testnet.

InvoiceNFT.sol

ERC-721 invoice tokenization with immutable lifecycle state.

LendingPool.sol

Dual funding model: direct invoice funding + pooled liquidity.

MockStablecoin.sol

Test token for deterministic end-to-end demo execution.

Current scope

  • On-chain data: marketplace, funding events, repayment events, pool balances.
  • Local-first modules: drafts, inventory, and KYB state for fast MVP iteration.
  • Next: stronger risk model, syndication support, and mainnet compliance layer.
